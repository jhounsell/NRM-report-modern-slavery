Last contact between the potential victim and their exploiters

To assess the risk that the potential victim may be exploited or trafficked again, we need to know about any communication between them and their exploiters.

You can provide information about how they communicated, what was said and who was involved.

Include details about any threats the exploiters have made. If applicable, describe any steps the potential victim has taken to prevent contact with their exploiters.
